Concured

Concured is using artificial intelligence (AI) to analyse millions of data points to highlight trending topic gaps for users or organisations to exploit. Concured not only knows what topics will engage an audience, it also informs the user what to write about to stand out from the crowd. In this way, it enables companies to be ahead of their competitors and be true thought-leaders.
